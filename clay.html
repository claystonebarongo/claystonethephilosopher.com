About Me

I am Claystone Barongo, and I came into this world in the year 2004, amidst the lush greenery of Kisii County, Kenya. From my earliest days, a profound love for technology has burned within me, guiding my every step on this journey called life. Growing up surrounded by the vibrant culture and warmth of Kisii, I found myself drawn to the endless possibilities that technology presented. With each passing day, my curiosity grew, propelling me to explore the intricate workings of the digital realm. As I journeyed through my formative years, my passion for technology only deepened. From tinkering with gadgets to diving into the world of coding and software development, I embraced every opportunity to expand my knowledge and skills. Driven by a relentless determination to make a difference, I ventured into various avenues within the tech industry. From artificial intelligence to cybersecurity, I sought to push the boundaries of innovation and leave my mark on the world. Through dedication and perseverance, I earned recognition as a rising star in the tech world. Yet, my journey is far from over. With each new day, I am inspired to continue pushing the limits of what is possible, fueled by a desire to create a better future for all. As I look ahead, I am filled with excitement for the adventures that await. With every challenge that comes my way, I am reminded of the boundless potential that lies within each of us to shape our destinies and change the world for the better. In the end, my story is not just mine alone—it is a testament to the power of passion, perseverance, and the belief that anything is possible when we dare to dream big and chase after those dreams with all our hearts.

Biography

In the intricate tapestry of my life, I am Claystone Barongo—a spoken word artist whose journey intertwines with the vibrant threads of literature, family, and relentless self-expression. Born amidst Kisii County's lush landscapes in 2004, alongside my twin brother, Stoneclay, the allure of the written word captivated me from early on, kindling a passion for poetry and prose that shaped my very existence. In the embrace of solitude, I sought solace within the pages of novels, finding guidance in the works of literary giants like Chinua Achebe and Ken Walibora. Yet, my journey was not solitary; it was nurtured by the sturdy roots of family and community. My parents, Elijah Mbweke, a respected figure in local politics, and Doris Rabera, whose love and strength sustained me, laid the foundation for my aspirations. Though grief shadowed my path with the loss of my mother, her memory became a beacon of purpose. Fuelled by ambition and the legacy of my forebears, I embarked on a quest to leave my mark on the world. In the mosaic of my interests, the allure of leadership and governance beckoned, influenced by political stalwarts like James Orengo and Raila Odinga. Initially drawn towards medicine or law, recognizing their potential for impact, I was inspired by figures such as PLO Lumumba and Ben Carson. However, it was software engineering that ultimately ignited my passion—a field where creativity meets innovation, echoing the rhythmic cadence of spoken word poetry. Alongside my siblings—Barrack, Brian Ayienda, Austine Mogonchi, Francis M. Bweke, Verah Nyatuka, and Violet Moraa—I embarked on a journey of self-discovery and collective growth, bound by the ties of blood and kinship. As I stand poised at the threshold of tomorrow, I am grateful for the winding path that has led me here. Each twist and turn, every triumph and tribulation, has shaped me into the person I am—a poet, a dreamer, a seeker of truth. And while my journey may have taken unexpected turns, I embrace the path I've chosen—a tapestry woven from the threads of love, loss, and the resilience of the human spirit, guided by a purpose greater than I could have imagined.

Education

Embarking on my educational journey in 2008 at Hezina Primary School, I took my first steps into the world of learning. However, it was at Malacare Memorial School where I undertook my KCPE exams in 2018, leaving an indelible mark with a stellar performance, clinching an A grade with an impressive 375 marks. This achievement showcased my dedication and thirst for knowledge right from the outset. Transitioning to secondary education at St. Edward Nyabioto High School, I continued to carve my path, consistently ranking at the top of my class. Yet, amidst the academic rigors, I found myself drawn to the captivating world of software engineering, a passion instilled by my brother Barack, to whom I owe a debt of gratitude that transcends words. My journey of leadership commenced during my formative years at Malacare Memorial School and later at St. Edward Nyabioto, where I had the honor of serving as the school president. These leadership roles not only nurtured my sense of responsibility but also fostered a deep-seated commitment to making a positive impact within my school community. Now, as I navigate the halls of Kirinyaga University, pursuing a degree in software engineering, I remain deeply engaged in campus politics. My tenure as a student leader persists, as I channel my energy into advocating for student welfare and championing initiatives that foster a vibrant academic environment. Each step of my educational journey, from Hezina Primary School to Malacare Memorial School, from St. Edward Nyabioto High School to Kirinyaga University, has been characterized by a relentless pursuit of excellence, fueled by a passion for learning and a commitment to leadership and service.

Interests and hobbies

Transitioning beyond my academic endeavors, my interests and hobbies reflect a diverse tapestry of passions and pursuits. At the core of my extracurricular activities lies a fervent enthusiasm for technology and innovation. Fueled by my fascination with software engineering, I spend countless hours delving into coding projects, exploring new software applications, and staying abreast of the latest technological advancements. This journey of tech exploration not only satiates my curiosity but also serves as a platform for creative expression and problem-solving. In addition to my love for technology, I find solace in the world of literature. Reading novels transports me to different realms, allowing me to explore diverse perspectives and immerse myself in captivating narratives. Whether it's delving into the complexities of a gripping thriller or savoring the lyrical prose of a classic novel, each page turn enriches my imagination and broadens my understanding of the human experience. Furthermore, I have discovered a passion for writing poetry, a form of artistic expression that allows me to channel my thoughts and emotions into words. Crafting verses that capture moments of beauty, introspection, and reflection provides me with a sense of catharsis and creative fulfillment. Whether it's penning verses that celebrate the wonders of nature or exploring the depths of human emotion, writing poetry enables me to connect with others on a profound and intimate level. Beyond my literary pursuits, my engagement in leadership roles has been a cornerstone of my personal development. Whether serving as the school president in both primary and secondary school or actively participating in campus politics at university, I am driven by a deep-seated commitment to advocacy and community engagement. This involvement not only hones my leadership skills but also instills in me a sense of responsibility towards effecting positive change within my academic and social spheres. In my leisure time, I relish the art of public speaking and communication. Whether it's engaging in debates, delivering speeches, or facilitating discussions, I seize every opportunity to articulate my thoughts and ideas on various subjects. This pursuit not only sharpens my oratory skills but also fosters meaningful dialogue and intellectual exchange. In essence, my hobbies and interests form the tapestry of my identity, weaving together strands of technology, literature, poetry, leadership, and communication. It is through these endeavors that I seek to cultivate a rich and fulfilling life, driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a profound commitment to making a positive impact in the world around me.
